    Education and Learning

    Registration for All India Pre-Medical Scholarship Entrance Test

    The AIPMT portal allows candidates to register online for the pre-medical test, make payments, receive admit cards, and fill OMR sheets, simplifying the examination process.

    Apply Online Scholarships for Meritorious Students belonging to the Minority Communities

    This service awards interest subsidies on education loans for minority community students pursuing overseas studies. It aims to promote higher education among underrepresented groups.

    Social Welfare Residential Educational Institutions Society (TSWREIS) - Information, Telangana

    Providing educational opportunities for marginalized communities, TSWREIS operates residential schools. Their website offers insights into the society's initiatives, fostering inclusive and quality education from primary to intermediate levels for SC/ST/BC students.

    Health and Wellness

    Health Establishment Registration for TB Control Ni-Kshay

    Ni-Kshay is a TB control program that monitors and tracks TB cases. It offers alerts for medication and follow-ups to both patients and healthcare providers.

    Find Nearest Blood Bank, eRaktKosh

    e-Rakt Kosh connects users to the nearest blood bank, streamlining the process of locating blood supplies and supporting the national blood bank network.

    Travel and Tourism

    Check Bus Driver Information, Telangana

    Citizens can make use of this service to check bus driver information the tickets booked in the TSRTC Telangana.

    Cancelled of Bus Ticket Services, Telangana

    Access information regarding cancelled tickets services offered by TSRTC, aiding passengers in understanding policies and procedures related to ticket cancellations.
